Watermelon Shapes

Watermelons, due to their round nature have a low packing efficiency. As such they take up a lot of unnecessary space in the refrigerator (or any other place where watermelons are stored). This is not a problem if you have excess space, but if you lack the space or just don't want your watermelon to roll, there are watermelon in a variety of shapes to suit your needs.
