The Story

Warren the Watermelon was wandering around Worland Wyoming when Wally, one of Warren's Wacky friends, waddled in front of wayward wagon.

"Watch Out Wally!", Warren bellowed.

Wally weaved out of the way without a minute to spare.

"Wow! Thanks Warren!" Willy Wailed, "What would I do without you?"
