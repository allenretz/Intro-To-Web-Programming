The Introduction

All of these pages are about watermelons. When I was originally making this project, I was going to make it set of Do's and Dont's for creating a website. However, three of the colors I chose in my theme reminded me of a watermelon. As Such I decided to make my project more whimsical and make it entirely about watermelons. First, I created a short story about a watermelon named Warren. Secondly, researched the history of the watermelon. Finally, I created a game about watermelons.
