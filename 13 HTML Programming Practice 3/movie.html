Welcome

Welcome to another Fabulous City Review. Today we are looking at the city that rests just north of the Missouri-Iowa Border along I-35. Some Notable Points of Interest are the Linden Street Coffee House, The Coliseum,Graceland University, the Livestock Auction, and the Lamoni Community Center.
